Privacy Policy

Dear users, our App focuses on helping users track expenses, income, budget and financial goals so that users can have a clear understanding of their personal or family financial situation. Through our app, users can conveniently record and categorize every expense and income, create personalized budgets, set financial goals, and monitor financial status in real time. Our goal is to enable users to manage their finances more effectively, make informed financial decisions, and achieve financial freedom and stability.


User authorization information and user input information in the application are only saved in the local sandbox of the application and will not be uploaded to the server or third party.


What information we collect.

Camera:

Syncspark will ask for permission to access your camera so you can fill in picture information on your bill, but this is not required. If you don't need this functionality, you can deny this permission.


Photo Gallery:

Syncspark will request permission to access your photo library so that you can fill in image information on your bill, but this is not required. If you don't need this functionality, you can deny this permission.


Address book:

Syncspark will ask for permission to access your address book so that you can add the appropriate contacts to your bill, but this is not required. If you don't need this functionality, you can deny this permission.


Location:

Syncspark will request access to your location so that you can include address information on your bill, but this is not required. If you don't need this functionality, you can deny this permission.


Log Data


We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.
